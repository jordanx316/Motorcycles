Motorcycle Riding Difficulty By Category/Style

1 Star ⭐ = Easiest - 5 Stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Hardest
Category Difficulty Description
Scooters ⭐ Easiest of the motorbikes to get into. Simple easy power delivery, and many offer automatic transmissions unlike most of the biles listed below.
Cruisers ⭐⭐ Low seat height & and comfortable riding position, cruisers are a step up from scooters in difficulty. Important to note cruisers vary widely and some can be far larger and heavyier than others.
Dual Sports ⭐⭐⭐ Sizes range but typically these bikes have a taller seat height and are a bit top heavy, as well as suited to off road riding which requires a bit of learning curve when compared to street riding.
Adventure Bikes ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Adventure bikes are quite large and probaly not the best bike for beginners. These bikes are well suited to moderate offroading.
Sport Bikes ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Sport bikes vary in size and usually have a forward leaing position that differs from the above listed bikes. Perhaps the biggest leaning curve comes from the power of these bikes. Most sport bikes are built for speed and power and not reccomended for those without proper training or experience.
